Overview

Luxury automotive seating, designed to be seen.

Modern Cushions manufactures and installs premium custom seat upholstery for vehicles across Nairobi. Their clientele is aspirational — buyers who want their car interiors to reflect the same quality as the rest of their lifestyle. The brief was clear: make everything look as premium as the product itself.

I came on board as the brand's full creative retainer — responsible for every visual touchpoint, from the daily Instagram grid to the massive banners that anchor their presence at trade expos and automotive shows across Kenya.

Print & Large Format

From fliers to 5-metre event banners.

Automotive expos in Kenya are high-energy, high-competition environments. Modern Cushions needed to cut through the noise with physical creative that communicated craftsmanship at a glance. I designed their complete large-format suite: tear-drop banners for showroom entrances, rollup displays for indoor booths, and 5-metre horizontal event banners that anchored their outdoor presence.

Every piece was designed at full print resolution in Canva Premium with CMYK-optimised colour profiles, ensuring the oxblood and gold palette reproduced faithfully under harsh outdoor lighting and indoor halogen spotlights alike.

Web & Seat Graphics

A website that sells the feel, not just the specs.

The Modern Cushions website was designed to function as an aspirational catalogue — premium photography layouts, smooth scroll, and a clear enquiry flow. I also designed custom graphics applied directly to seat samples for display purposes, turning physical products into visual marketing assets.

The site was built no-code with a focus on mobile performance, since the majority of their audience discovers them via Instagram and expects a seamless mobile browsing experience before making an enquiry call.
