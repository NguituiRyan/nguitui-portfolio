Overview

Leadership deserves a visual voice that commands respect.

The Strathmore Presidential Subcommittee is the highest student leadership body at the university — responsible for representing the student body and coordinating major institutional events. Their communications needed to carry the weight of that position: professional, warm, and distinctly Strathmore.

I was engaged for seasonal creative and event video documentation — producing Christmas and New Year greeting posters distributed across the university's communication channels, and recording video content at the committee's formal gatherings.

Seasonal Creative

Greetings that feel considered, not generic.

Holiday greetings from institutional bodies often feel like afterthoughts — stock imagery, generic copy, a committee name dropped in at the bottom. The brief here was the opposite: create seasonal posters that felt genuinely crafted, that students would actually stop and read rather than scroll past.

The Christmas and New Year poster series used a rich maroon and gold palette — anchored in warmth and celebration — with custom typographic layouts that elevated the committee's messaging into something worth sharing. The designs were optimised for both WhatsApp status formats and full-size notice board prints.
