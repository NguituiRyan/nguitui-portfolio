Overview

Financial credibility, built from the first impression.

Wealth Wave operates in Kenya's rapidly growing Forex and financial education space — a market where trust is everything and where the visual quality of a brand signals credibility before a single word is read. They needed a logo and brand system that could stand alongside established financial institutions while communicating accessibility and opportunity to a younger, entrepreneurially-minded audience.

The project centred on two deliverables: a complete logo and brand identity system, and event poster creative for their Forex Trade Expo — a major gathering of traders, analysts, and financial educators in Nairobi.

Brand Identity

Gold that earns its place — not borrowed luxury.

The Wealth Wave logomark abstracts a rising wave into a chart movement — a double reference that speaks directly to both the finance and prosperity dimensions of the brand name. The primary gold palette was chosen carefully: not the garish yellow-gold of low-end financial brands, but a muted, deep gold that reads as earned rather than aspirational.

A dark charcoal base provides contrast and seriousness, preventing the identity from reading as flashy or speculative. The overall system communicates something important in the Kenyan Forex market: this is a brand worth trusting with financial decisions.

Forex Trade Expo

Event creative that commands a room before the room fills.

The Forex Trade Expo poster series had to perform across multiple formats simultaneously: digital promotional posts shared in trader WhatsApp groups and on Instagram, physical banners at the event venue, and projected title slides used during the sessions themselves. A unified visual language across all formats was essential.

The poster design language drew from financial data visualisation: clean grid structures, data-point typography treatments, and subtle chart-line motifs embedded in the background geometry. The effect was sophisticated without being cold — the kind of event you want your peers to know you attended.
