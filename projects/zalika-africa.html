Zalika
Africa

A complete brand and digital system for a Nairobi-based caregiver training school — logo through to website and a custom school management system.

RoleWeb Designer & Graphic Designer
ClientZalika Africa
Year2024 – Present
DeliverablesLogo · Banners · Cards · Website · School Management System

Overview

Professional caregiving starts with a professional brand.

Zalika Africa trains and certifies caregivers across Kenya — a profession that carries enormous responsibility and deserves to be presented with corresponding seriousness and warmth. The school needed a visual identity that communicated trustworthiness to both students and the families who would employ their graduates.

I led the end-to-end brand build: from the initial logo concept through to the physical print collateral used at their open days, and ultimately the website and a custom school management system for tracking enrolments, schedules, and certifications.

Print Collateral

Every touchpoint tells the same story.

Zalika Africa's physical presence — at community health fairs, hospital notice boards, and open-day events — needed to match the professionalism of the digital brand. I designed their complete print suite: roll-up banners and tear-drop banners for indoor and outdoor events, 3-metre event banners for large venue entrances, business cards, fliers, and a multi-page information brochure for prospective students.

The teal-and-forest-green palette was chosen to reference health and growth — colours the target audience associates with medical credibility and natural care. Each piece was produced at full print resolution and submitted to local print vendors with bleed marks and colour-safe exports.

Digital

Website + custom school management system.

The Zalika Africa website serves two audiences simultaneously: prospective students looking for credible training, and families seeking certified caregivers. The information architecture was designed to speak to both without confusion — clear pathways to course information and a simple enquiry form for placement requests.

Beyond the website, I designed the UI for a custom school management system: a lightweight internal tool for tracking student enrolments, session schedules, attendance, and certification records. The system reduced admin overhead significantly and gave the school a more professional operational backbone.
